INTRODUCTION

Every nation on Earth is now after the keys to control AI technology The Entity. For Ethan, nothing matters more than reaching them first so he can destroy them, rather than letting governments try and harness forces they don't understand.
His quest takes him to icy research stations, in the cockpits of biplanes soaring through canyons, and deep under the ocean's surface as Cruise showcase another ridiculous array of stunts.
